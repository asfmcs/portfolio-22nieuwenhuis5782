Travel to Madagascar!

By: Cristy Nieuwenhuis

Basic Information

Madagascar is the place of your dreams! It has incredable landscapes, it
has beautiful beaches, amazing forests, and breathtaking rock formation
landscapes. The culture in this island is very different since there is a mix
of people and arts.

People

The people you find in this island is very diverse, since in Madagascar
there was British, French, Indian and Chinese influences. Madagascar is
a mix of Southeast Asia and East Afica. People who live in rural areas mostly
plant crops, and do that for a living. But, there is also a urban area were
people go to work like in any other country.

Art

In Madagascar people make beutiful art, they make some weavings from
silk and cloth. They also make beautiful wood carving, such as decorative
vases and furniture. Many of their artwork is inspired by Asian and African
cultures mixed together to create a piece of art.

Tourist Places

with little braches (the one in the picture below to the left). Then there is

Beaches

Now, lets talk about the beaches, some of them are:

  • Ile aux Nattes, Sainte-Marie:
    This beach is a small island next to Madagascar, you get there by crossing a small channel, con a canoe. The island is very close to Madagascar.
  • Anakao:
    This beach is a coastal fishing town, on this beach you can arrange a trip to go whale watching.
  • Nosy Iranja:
    In this island, you can connect from one island to another by crossing a beach that has water from both sides, and their is a sand trail in teh middle of the ocean to cross from island to island.

Top 5 Things to do in Madagascar

  1. Go to any beach!
  2. Go to the Baobab Trees
  3. Scuba Dive

  5. Explore the forests!
